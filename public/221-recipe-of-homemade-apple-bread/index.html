The ingredients needed to make Apple bread:
  1. Get 2 loaf pans
  2. Take 1 cooking spray
  3. Take 3 cup flour
  4. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Take 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Prepare 1 cup vegetable oil
  8. Take 1 cup brown sugar
  9. Prepare 1 cup granulated sugar
  10. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  11. Prepare 8 oz sour cream
  12. Get 3 eggs
  13. Get 4 apples

Steps to make Apple bread:
  1. Preheat the oven to 315°F. Then prepare the loaf pans with the cooking spray
  2. Chop all the apples into small/medium chunks. Save the last apple and cut into slices to lay on top of the bread (optional)
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda, salt , and cinnamon in a large bowl
  4. Lightly beat the vegetable oil, brown sugar, white sugar, vanilla, sour cream, and eggs together in a separate large bowl
  5. Fold in and mix the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients. Then mix in the chopped apples
  6. Evenly divide mixture in the 2 pans and bake in the preheated oven for about 80 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
  7. Let the loafs cool and enjoy!
