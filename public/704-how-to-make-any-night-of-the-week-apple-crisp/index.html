Steps to make Apple Crisp:
  1. Preheat oven to 375. Peel, core, and slice apples. Sprinkle the other filling ingredients over the apples, stir to combine, and pour into 13x9 baking dish.
  2. Prepare topping: melt butter in microwave. Stir oats, flour, brown sugar, and salt together with a fork. Add in the butter and stir with fork until it's combined and crumbly. Put over top of the apple mixture.
  3. Bake for 30 minutes until filling is bubbly and topping is browned, turning once halfway through. Serve with vanilla ice cream if desired (or, as pictured, caramel swirl ice cream).
