1. Make ready 3/4 cup brown sugar

1. Get 1/2 cup quick oats
1. Get 1/3 cup butter
1. Prepare 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon




##### Steps to make Apple Crisp - with Oatmeal Topping:

1. Heat oven to375° .
