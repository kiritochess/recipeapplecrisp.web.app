The ingredients needed to make Apple Cake in a Bread Machine:
  1. Take 100 grams Unsalted butter
  2. Get 4 tbsp Light brown sugar (or white sugar)
  3. Get 1 tbsp Milk
  4. Prepare 2 Beaten eggs
  5. Get 200 grams Pancake mix
  6. Get For the apple filling:
  7. Take 1/2 Apple
  8. Prepare 5 grams Butter
  9. Take 1 tbsp Light brown sugar (or white sugar)
  10. Make ready Topping:
  11. Prepare 1 Whipped cream (optional)
Instructions to make Apple Cake in a Bread Machine:
  1. Make the apple filling. Cut the apple into 1cm pieces.
  2. Put the diced apple, sugar and butter in a pan and stir fry.
  3. When the butter and sugar have melted and everything is mixed well, simmer over low-medium heat until the moisture in the pan is gone, stirring occasionally.
  4. When the moisture in the pan is gone and the apple pieces are translucent, lightly browned and shiny, the filling is done.
  5. Prepare the other ingredients. Let the unsalted butter come to room temperature, and cut into small pieces.
  6. Put the ingredients in the order listed in the bread machine. (Butter → sugar → milk → eggs → pancake mix → apple filling.)
  7. Choose the "cake" setting, and press the Start button.
  8. When the machine beeps, open the lid and scrape down the sides of the bread pan. Press the Start button again.
  9. Take the cake out of the bread pan as soon as it's done. Cool on a rack and it's done!!
