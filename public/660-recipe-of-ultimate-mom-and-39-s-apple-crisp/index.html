Steps to make Mom's Apple Crisp:
  1. Peel and core apples. Cut apples into 1/4 inch slices
  2. Toss apple slices in lemon juice and spread evenly in bottom of a greased 9 inch pie plate.
  3. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  4. In a medium mixing bowl add flour, brown sugar, rolled oats and cut butter/ margarine in with pastry cutter until topping resembles a course crumb mixture
  5. Sprinkle apples with white sugar and cinnamon
  6. Spread topping mixture evenly over apples and bake for 35- 45 min. or until topping is golden brown .
  7. Let cool for 15 min and serve with ice cream or table cream.
