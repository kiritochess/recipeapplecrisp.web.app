Instructions to make Apple Banana BOAT (Baked oatmeal):
  1. Pre heat oven to 375.
  2. Spray muffin pan with non stick cooking spray
  3. Mix BOAT ingredients in a bowl
  4. Peal and core Apple, then slice horizontally.
  5. Start layering with one piece of apple in the bottom of the pan.
  6. Add a layer of :BOAT mix, Banana, Apple, BOAT mix, Banana.
  7. Sprinkle a little splenda, spray with olive oil and place in oven.
  8. Bake at 375 for 10 minutes. Then turn to broil for 3 minutes to slightly brown
  9. Top with whipped cream and enjoy. One Apple Banana BOAT is 120 calories unless you leave out whipped cream then 105.
