The ingredients needed to make Crispy Apple Pie Fritters:
  1. Make ready Gyoza skins
  2. Prepare Apple
  3. Get Sugar
  4. Prepare Lemon juice
  5. Take Butter
  6. Make ready Cinnamon
  7. Prepare Frying oil
Steps to make Crispy Apple Pie Fritters:
  1. Cut the apple into 8 equal portions and cut into wedges.
  2. Add the apples, lemon juice, sugar, and butter to a pot, sprinkle with cinnamon to taste, and boil over low heat until the apple softens.
  3. Coat the edges of a gyoza skin with water, and wrap the apple compote just like wrapping up gyoza filling.
