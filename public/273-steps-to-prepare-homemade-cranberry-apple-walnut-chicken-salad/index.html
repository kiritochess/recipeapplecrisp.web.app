The ingredients needed to make Cranberry Apple Walnut Chicken Salad:
  1. Take 3 can Chicken (pure white meat)
  2. Get 3 Apple's (peeled and chopped)
  3. Take 3 tbsp dried cranberries
  4. Get 1 cup mayonnaise
  5. Get 1 bunch chopped walnuts
  6. Get 5 chopped celery
  7. Get 1 bunch cut green/white grapes
  8. Make ready 1 chopped green bell pepper
  9. Prepare 2 1/2 dash ground black pepper
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp honey
  11. Get 1 tsp paprika
  12. Take 1 dash kosher salt
  13. Take 1 loaf ciabatta or desired bread
Instructions to make Cranberry Apple Walnut Chicken Salad:
  1. Drain chicken and pour into bowl/air tight container. Shred chicken to desired size.
  2. Add all ingredients and mix together.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Serve on toasted ciabatta bread or bread of choice and enjoy!
