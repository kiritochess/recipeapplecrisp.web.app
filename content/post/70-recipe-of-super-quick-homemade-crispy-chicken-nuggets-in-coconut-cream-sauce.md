##### The ingredients needed to make Crispy Chicken Nuggets in Coconut Cream Sauce:

1. Get 2 T. sugar

1. Take  _____________________

1. Make ready  Cooked rice




##### Instructions to make Crispy Chicken Nuggets in Coconut Cream Sauce:
