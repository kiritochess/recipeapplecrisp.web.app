The ingredients needed to make Irenea's Apple Tart:
  1. Get 150 grams butter
  2. Take 70 grams powdered sugar
  3. Make ready 3 medium eggs
  4. Make ready 1 medium lemon or lime
  5. Make ready 450 grams Apple (crispy & tart)
  6. Get 150 grams orange marmalade
  7. Take 200 grams all purpose flour
Steps to make Irenea's Apple Tart:
  1. Preheat oven 180C
  2. Mix flour, butter (cut in small cubes), lemon rinds
  3. Add 2 egg yolks
  4. Mix until you form into a ball
  5. Put in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap & refrigerate
  6. Divide into 2 & roll out on a lightly floured surface
  7. Put at bottom of pan & leave some excess at the edge
  8. Bake 15-20 minutes
  9. Meantime, core & cut apple into slivers & add the lemon juice
  10. Alternate layers: apple slices then thin layer of orange marmalade then apples last
  11. Cover with the 2nd dough & pinch edges closed
  12. Warm an egg with some milk & brush this over the crust
  13. Make a crisscut on top so that the inside doesnt pop when it is getting baked
  14. Bake 30 minutes till golden brown
  15. Cool
  16. Put on a serving dish (we like this with a cup of freshly brewed coffee)
