Steps to make Crispy applesauce:
  1. Cut up three apples into very small squares
  2. In large pan, melt butter,sprinkle dash of salt, add vanilla, stir.
  3. Add nutmeg, cinnamon, and light corn syrup, stir.
  4. Add sugar, brown sugar, stir well.
  5. Add in all apples, stir well to get the "sauce" all over the apples.
  6. Add three handfuls (bunches) of oats and stir well to get it all mixed, then put top on and let sit on low for about 5-10 minutes. Take out of pan, let cool, stir and enjoy.
