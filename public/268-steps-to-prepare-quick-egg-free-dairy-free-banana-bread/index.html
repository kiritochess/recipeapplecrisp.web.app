The ingredients needed to make Egg-free, Dairy-free Banana Bread:
  1. Take 4 bananas
  2. Take 1/2 cup unflavored oil (Carnola oil, Sunflower seed oil, ect)
  3. Prepare 300 ml soy milk
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 3 cup all-purpose flour
  6. Take 2 tbsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 1 cup chopped walnuts (or your favourite nuts)
  8. Prepare dried fruit (optional, I used slice apple)

Instructions to make Egg-free, Dairy-free Banana Bread:
  1. Mash 3 bananas and slice 1 banana
  2. Mix mashed banana with oil, soy milk and sugar
  3. Sieve in the flour and baking powder. Stir everything together with rubber spatula.
  4. Add sliced banana, nuts and dried frite.
  5. Pour the batter in loaf pan lined with baking paper or aluminium foil cup. Bake at 180°C for 30-45 minutes.
  6. Keep the banana bread 24hr before serve for better taste.
