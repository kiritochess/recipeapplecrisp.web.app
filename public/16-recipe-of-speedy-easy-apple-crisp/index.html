The ingredients needed to make Easy Apple Crisp:
  1. Prepare 2-3 cans apple pie filling
  2. Make ready 1 box yellow or white cake mix
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup melted butter

Instructions to make Easy Apple Crisp:
  1. Pre heat oven to 350°F, grease a 13 x 9 pan. Put the cans of apple pie filling in the bottom of pan
  2. Spread the cake mix evenly on top. (Do not prepare the cake , just use the powder.)
  3. Pour the melted butter on top of cake mix, try getting it everywhere.
  4. Put in oven and cook for 40-50 minutes, or until bubbly. Best served with vanilla ice cream
