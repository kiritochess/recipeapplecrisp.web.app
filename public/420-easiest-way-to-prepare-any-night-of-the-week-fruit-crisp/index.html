The ingredients needed to make Fruit Crisp:
  1. Get 5 cup apples, peeled & sliced (or pears, peaches)
  2. Take 3 tbsp sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 old fashioned rolled oats
  4. Make ready 1/2 brown sugar
  5. Take 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped nuts (or additional oats)

This fruit crisp recipe lets you use almost any fruit. Apple crisp, peach cobbler, cherry crisp, blueberry cobbler. Pick a fruit and use this recipe to make it. No need to have fresh fruit on hand to pull together this classic cobbler.

Steps to make Fruit Crisp:
  1. Place fruit in 8x8 baking dish and toss with 2-4 tbsp sugar.
  2. Preheat oven to 375.
  3. In medium bowl, combine oats, brown sugar, flour, and spices. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in nuts if using. Sprinkle topping over fruit.
  4. Bake at 375 for 30-35 minutes until golden brown. Serve warm w/ vanilla ice cream!
