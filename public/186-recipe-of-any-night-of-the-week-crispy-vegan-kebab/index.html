Steps to make Crispy Vegan Kebab:
  1. Take a bowl, mix all the ingredients in it so well that it becomes a good dough.
  2. Now take small amount of your dough and give shapes to it and keep it aside.
  3. For Frying, take a pan, pour some oil in it and let it heat on low-medium flame.
  4. As the oil is ready, one by one put your shaped kebabs in it carefully.
  5. Fry till it turns brown from both sides. - Your kebab is ready, serve it with sauce or chutney.
