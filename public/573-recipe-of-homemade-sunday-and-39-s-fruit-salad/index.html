The ingredients needed to make Sunday's Fruit Salad:
  1. Prepare 4 large honey crisp apples
  2. Make ready 1-1/3 cup toasted walnuts
  3. Prepare 2 large navel orange plus the juices
  4. Get 1-1/2 cup seedless grapes sliced in half
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup maple syrup
  6. Make ready 8 ounces container of cool whip or whipped cream
  7. Prepare 8 cherries for the toppings
