The ingredients needed to make Easy peasy apple crisp!:
  1. Take 6 cup pealed & sliced apples (about 4 large apples - a mix of granny smith and ambrosia are delicious!)
  2. Get 1 1/2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  3. Get 2 tbsp honey
  4. Get 6 tbsp butter or margarine
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup quick cooking oats
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  9. Take 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

Instructions to make Easy peasy apple crisp!:
  1. Combine apples and 1-1/2 tbls flour in a round baking dish. Mix honey and set aside.
  2. Microwave butter on high in glass mixing bowl for 15 to 30 seconds. Mix in remaining ingredients with fork until crumbly. Sprinkle over apples.
  3. Bake at 350degreesF for 50 to 60 minutes.
