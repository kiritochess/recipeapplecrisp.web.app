The ingredients needed to make Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal:
  1. Take 1 large apple (I used honey crisp)
  2. Prepare 1/2 Cup old fashioned oats
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon brown sugar (or maple syrup)
  4. Take 1 Cup milk of choice
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  6. Take 1 teaspoon cinammon
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Get 1/4 Cup apple sauce (optional)
  9. Get Chopped walnuts for garnish
  10. Get Butter for pan

Steps to make Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal:
  1. Chop apple into small chunks
  2. Heat butter in medium sized pot on high heat. Once melted, add apples and brown sugar, cinnamon, apple sauce, and nutmeg. Stir until evenly coated.
  3. Pour in oats, milk, and vanilla extract and set to boil. Once boiling, continue to stir. If oats are too wet for preference add oats; too dry, add more milk. Reduce to medium heat and cover for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve immediately with walnuts and more cinnamon. Happy Fall!
