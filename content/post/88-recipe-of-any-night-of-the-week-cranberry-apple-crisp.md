1. Take 1/4 cup sugar
1. Prepare 3 tsp cinnamon
1. Make ready 1 tsp nutmeg
1. Get  second part
1. Get 1/3 cup quick oats

1. Prepare 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed

##### Steps to make cranberry Apple Crisp:
