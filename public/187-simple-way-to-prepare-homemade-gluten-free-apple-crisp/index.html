The ingredients needed to make Gluten Free Apple Crisp:
  1. Get 6 cup Apple's-sliced
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  3. Take 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup All Purpose Gf flour
  5. Take 1/2 cup Gf OATS
  6. Make ready 3/4 tsp Cinnamon
  7. Make ready 3/4 tsp Nutmeg
  8. Get 1/3 cup Soft Butter
  9. Get 1 tsp Xanthan Gum
