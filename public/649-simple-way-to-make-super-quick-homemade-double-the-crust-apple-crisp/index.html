Steps to make Double The Crust Apple Crisp:
  1. Set oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease a 13 by 9 pan.
  3. Peel, core and slice apples.
  4. In a mixing bowl combine apples, ground cinnamon, salt and sugar. Mix well.
  5. Place apple mixture evenly across the bottom of the pan.
  6. In the same mixing bowl mix oats, brown sugar, flour and butter until mixed all the way through. Should be crumbly.
  7. Apply the crumble mixture evenly over apples and place in oven.
  8. Bake for 50 minutes. Done!
